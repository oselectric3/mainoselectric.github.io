Who We Are

O.S. Electric is a family-owned and operated electrical contractor based in Buffalo, New York. Founded in 1992, we have built our reputation on quality workmanship, professional service, and a strong commitment to safety and electrical code compliance.

With decades of hands-on experience, our team of licensed Master and Journeyman electricians delivers dependable electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and specialized facilities throughout the Buffalo area.

Our Experience & Approach

At O.S. Electric, all work is performed by licensed Master and Journeyman electricians. We do not cut corners, rush projects, or rely on unqualified labor. Every job is completed with attention to detail, adherence to current electrical codes, and a focus on long-term reliability.

We understand that electrical systems are critical to safety, daily operations, and property protection. That is why we take a proactive, solutions-based approach to troubleshooting, repairs, and system upgrades.

Why Choose O.S. Electric

What sets O.S. Electric apart is our combination of experience, professionalism, and personal service. As a locally owned electrical contractor, we are able to build lasting relationships with our customers and provide responsive, dependable service when it matters most.

Our commitment to safety, code compliance, and quality workmanship has earned the trust of homeowners, businesses, property managers, and specialized facilities throughout the Buffalo region.

Serving Buffalo & Surrounding Areas

We proudly serve residential homeowners, commercial businesses, property managers, and institutional clients throughout Buffalo, NY and surrounding communities. From small service calls to complex electrical systems, O.S. Electric delivers reliable solutions built to last.
